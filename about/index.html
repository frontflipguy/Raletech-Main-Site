About the Puzzles
These puzzles are presented without any rules or clues for solving them, so it's up to you to figure them out. My advice for finding the solution is that you use trial and error and think outside of the box. Also, I realize that it can be really frustrating if you're just not on the same wavelength as I was when I thought up the puzzles. I totally understand that and there's no shame in that. There is a hint button in the top right corner of every puzzle to help you out.
About the 2-player Games
These games are for 1-on-1 competition. You and a friend (or yourself, if you have no friends like me) can play these games together. The rules governing these games are simple and can be understood quickly, but the best strategies for winning may take more time to figure out. I hope that you find these games interesting and worth playing.
